Just create your own website! That is after all what I did.

 17-04-2022  253 words   2 minutes 
Contents
  • And that is exactly what happened to me
  • And maybe the same will happen to you

Recently a peer [1] from university showed me the polyring.ch website. If you are not familiar with it consider to check it out! Basically it is website to link together a set of private websites (mostly IT blogs) from CS students at ETH. I personally love to see self-made web content especially if it is made by my peers at university. Firstly is really neat to learn about what people in your studies are working on and reading how they solved a problem. But most importantly it gives you inspiration for your own projects.

And that is exactly what happened to me

This site is heavily inspired by the website from Mark Csurgay. Just like I did you can check out his posts and read about how to set up a static website with Hugo. The post is called “Getting startet with Hugo”.

And maybe the same will happen to you

Setting up a simple page like this one turned out to be not that difficult. I spend an evening to decide for a Hugo theme, configuring it and hosting the page but all other steps were no big deal. If you feel like I did after seeing similar content (super motivated) then why not just try it out yourself. The only frustrating thing that can (and probably will) happen is that nothing works at the beginning. You may bang your head on the table but other than that: I recommend you to learn new stuff and try it out.

[1]: Thanks Oliver!
